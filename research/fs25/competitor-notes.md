# Competitor / Existing Mod Notes

## Hemp Production System V2.0

Reference:
https://fs25.net/hemp-production-system-v1-1/

Notes:
- Production-focused hemp system.
- Includes greenhouse, hemp factory, bakery, tailor, rope factory.
- Outputs include hemp oil, fabric, paper, rope, twine, clothes, and food products.
- Uses palletized items.
- Mentions changed filltype names for compatibility with mod maps that already include hemp.

Project Decision:
FS25 Hemp Industries will avoid copying this structure directly.
Our focus will be industrial hemp agriculture, field crop gameplay, greenhouse production, sustainable materials, and custom equipment.
