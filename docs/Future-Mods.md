# Future Mod Concepts

## Contractor Equipment Rental Yard

### Core Idea

A console-friendly placeable contractor headquarters that gives players access to high-capacity commercial equipment and pays crop-delivery bonuses through normal XML-driven production mechanics.

### Player Fantasy

The player operates a high-tier agricultural contracting company rather than a small farm. The yard includes equipment parking, workshop bays, fuel and repair areas, crop intake points, a contractor office, and specialized contractor-edition machinery.

### First Crossplay-Safe Version

- Separate mod from Green Horizon Industries.
- XML/I3D-focused design with no required custom scripts.
- Contractor yard configured as a production or selling facility.
- Crop delivery recipes styled as commercial contract-completion bonuses.
- High-capacity contractor-edition harvesters, headers, trailers, and trucks.
- Low lease cost, expensive ownership, and meaningful upkeep instead of unrestricted free equipment.
- Daily processing limits and crop-specific payout balancing to avoid an unlimited-money exploit.

### Important Limitations

- A normal crop intake cannot prove whether crops came from a mission field or the player's own field.
- XML alone probably cannot restrict equipment to active contracts.
- Standard harvesting missions may require delivery to their assigned destination rather than the custom yard.
- True active-contract detection, timed rentals, and equipment locks would likely require a separate PC-scripted edition.

### Possible Project Names

### Development Gate

Do not begin production work until Green Horizon Industries has a working in-game greenhouse model and a stable packaging/testing workflow.
